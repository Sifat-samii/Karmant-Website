Dhaka, Bangladesh-based Thrash metal band **Karmant** have released their debut EP titled _Riot in Uniform_. The title track, _Riot in Uniform_, was inspired by the nationwide student movement of 2018. More info on the student movement here:

https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/2018_Bangladesh_road-safety_protests

Formed in 2016, **Karmant** is a four-piece consisting of Zami Hossain on Vocals & Bass, Hasan Zamil & Rumman Sharier on guitars & Naweed Kabir on drums. The band draws most of its inspiration from the German teutonic trio. Lyrical themes include but are not limited to fictional characters & political inconsistencies.

_Riot in Uniform_ is an independent release & is available for streaming on all major platforms. Physical copies will be available sometime soon.
