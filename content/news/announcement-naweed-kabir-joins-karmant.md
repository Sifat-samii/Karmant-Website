Karmant is excited to announce the addition of Naweed Kabir as the new drummer. Naweed brings his ruthless and powerful drumming style to the band, adding a new dimension to Karmant's thrash metal sound.

With his aggressive fills and tight timing, Naweed's drumming provides the relentless rhythm section that drives Karmant's music forward. His addition to the lineup marks an important milestone in the band's evolution.
